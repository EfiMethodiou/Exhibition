ART & SCIENCE NODE

ASN provides a platform for the artistic, scientific and technological milieus. It develops and supports interdisciplinary projects, organizes international exhibitions, discussion panels and “artists in laboratories” residence programs and interdisciplinary workshops. ASN team, members and partners are professionals – theorists and practitioners from multiple organizations and institutions found all over the world. Public engagement and education are one of the most important challenges for the art and science communities. That is why ASN develops innovative e communication strategies for the benefit of the society of knowledge.
